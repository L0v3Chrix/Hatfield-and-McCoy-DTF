Specialty Prints

When standard DTF isn’t enough. Specialty finishes that make your designs impossible to ignore.

Glitter DTF Transfers

Silver • Gold • Multi-Color

Real metallic glitter embedded in every transfer. Not a filter. Not a simulation. These catch light from every angle and turn heads across any room. Available in silver, gold, and multi-color finishes on the 22" wide format.

  • Same pricing as standard 22" DTF
  • 22" wide, up to 300" long
  • Three finish options: Silver, Gold, Multi
  • Heat press application — same as standard
  • Durable wash-after-wash performance

Glow in the Dark DTF

Premium • Photoluminescent

Photoluminescent transfers that absorb light and glow for hours. Perfect for events, nightlife apparel, safety gear, kids’ clothing, and anyone who wants their designs working overtime — even when the lights go out.

  • Premium pricing (~1.5x standard)
  • 22" wide, up to 300" long
  • Charges under any light source
  • Hours of visible glow in darkness
  • Same easy heat press application

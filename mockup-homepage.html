Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about DTF transfers and working with us.

DTF (Direct-to-Film) is a transfer printing method where designs are printed onto a special PET film, coated with adhesive powder, and then heat-pressed onto fabric. It works on virtually any material — cotton, polyester, blends, nylon, leather, and more — without any pretreatment required.

We accept PNG, JPG, PDF, AI, and EPS files. For best results, submit high-resolution files (300 DPI minimum) with transparent backgrounds. If you're not sure about your file, just send it — we'll let you know if anything needs adjustment.

A gang sheet lets you pack multiple designs onto a single sheet, maximizing your print area and minimizing cost. Instead of ordering each design separately, you arrange them all on one sheet and pay by the sheet size, not per design. Our gang sheet builder makes this easy.

Standard orders ship within 1-3 business days after artwork approval. We ship via USPS Priority Mail, which typically delivers in 2-3 days. Rush options are available — just ask. Local Virginia customers can arrange pickup.

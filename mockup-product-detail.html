DTF (Direct-to-Film) is a printing method where designs are printed onto PET film, coated with adhesive powder, and heat-pressed onto fabric. It works on any material — cotton, polyester, blends, leather, nylon — without pretreatment.

  • 300 DPI minimum resolution
  • Transparent background (PNG) preferred
  • Accepted formats: PNG, JPG, PDF, AI, EPS
  • Max file size: 50MB
  • CMYK or RGB color mode
  • Mirror/flip is NOT required — we handle it

Standard turnaround is 1-3 business days after artwork approval. Rush options available. Orders placed before 12pm EST typically ship next business day.

We ship via USPS Priority Mail (2-3 day delivery). Free shipping on orders over $100. Local Virginia customers can arrange same-day pickup. Tracking provided on all orders.
